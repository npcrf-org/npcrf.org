title: "Contact"
description: "How to reach npcrf.org: info@npcrf.org, published in plain text."
---

Write to [**info@npcrf.org**](mailto:info@npcrf.org).

The address is published in plain text, with no contact form and no obfuscation, so that it remains accessible to everyone, including readers using screen readers, text-mode browsers or no JavaScript.

Contact is welcome for:

- **error reports** — factual mistakes in articles, handled under the [corrections policy](/methodology/);
- **source suggestions** — primary sources or documents relevant to a published or future article;
- **licensing questions** — reuse and attribution of content (see [License](/license/));
- general enquiries about the project.
