npcrf.org is a non-profit, non-governmental organization. Its mission is honest, factual education: establishing and presenting the factual truth about current affairs and historical subjects, with no room for information that cannot be verified.

## Editorial principles

1. **Facts before narrative.** Every article exists to establish what can be shown to be true, not to advance a viewpoint. Where the facts are incomplete, the article says so.
2. **Verifiability is the bar for publication.** A claim that cannot be traced to an identified, checkable source is not published. There are no exceptions for convenient or popular claims.
3. **Primary sources first.** Original records, documents, datasets and first-hand material take precedence over commentary. Secondary sources are used to provide context, not as the basis of fact.
4. **Independence.** The site carries no advertising and no sponsored content. It is funded exclusively by [donations](/support/), which buy no influence over what is published.
5. **Transparency.** Every article ends with its full list of sources. Errors are corrected openly, with a visible record of what changed. The content is openly licensed ([CC BY 4.0](/license/)) and the site's code is open source.
6. **Plain language.** Articles are written to be read: sober, precise and free of jargon wherever possible.

How these principles are applied in practice is described in the [methodology](/methodology/).
